Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Terminals

A leading provider of storage services for bulk liquids and gases

Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Terminals’ mission is to be the most respected global storage provider, delivering safe, high-quality storage and handling solutions for a variety of speciality bulk liquids, such as chemicals, clean petroleum products, liquefied petroleum gases, biofuels, vegetable oils, alternative fuels and feedstocks.


With 5 million cbm of storage capacity across 10 wholly owned and four joint-venture terminals, we give customers access to key international shipping and transportation hubs close to their operations.

Our services complement the capabilities of Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers and Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tank Containers, creating an efficient and flexible ship-to-shore solution that reduces potential demurrage costs and supports their agile and competitive supply chains.

Ready when you are, wherever you are

Our terminal network covers the globe, and every terminal is conveniently located with access to key international shipping hubs. This means our expert teams can quickly adapt to our customers’ diverse and changing needs, all within an environment of assured quality, safety and sustainable operations.

Responsibly handling your bulk liquid storage needs

At Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Terminals, we comply with, and often exceed, all regulatory requirements across all our operations. Our highly experienced team cares about the safe handling of our customers’ products and every employee and contractor receives training on local safety standards and requirements. We are proud of our award-winning safety record, but we know that we must never become complacent, and we can always do more.

Specialised tank cleaning and railcar services

Our terminals in Houston, USA offer highly specialised tank cleaning services at third-party facilities, including those requiring advanced cleaning techniques with vapour control.
With our experience and expertise in cleaning storage tanks at our own facilities, we can offer quick turnarounds and cost-efficient services, maintaining the highest safety standards, and ensure that almost any type of residue is thoroughly removed from your assets, meeting highest standard of cleanliness for storage. We also have experience of Kosher cleaning.
