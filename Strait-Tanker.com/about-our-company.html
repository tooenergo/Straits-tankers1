Who We Are

Who We Are

We are the world’s largest operator of sophisticated chemical tankers, a global provider of safe storage services for bulk liquids, and the leading provider of door-to-door transportation services for bulk-liquid chemicals and food-grade products. We are also pioneers in high-tech sustainable seafood production and investors in LNG shipping and distribution opportunities.

For more than 60 years,
the Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. name has been synonymous with delivering exceptional services in
bulk-liquid logistics,
distribution and high-quality
storage services.
