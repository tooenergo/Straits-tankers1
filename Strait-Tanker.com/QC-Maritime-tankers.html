Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers

The world’s largest operator of chemical tankers

Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers provides safe, sustainable, reliable and high-quality transportation services to the world’s leading manufacturers and consumers of chemicals, edible oils, acids and other bulk liquids.


We collaborate to create flexible and personalised solutions, and our culture of ongoing innovation ensures that Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers remains at the forefront of the industry.

We have the world’s largest fleet of chemical tankers. Our global deep-sea fleet – combined with our integrated regional capabilities in Europe, Asia Pacific, the Indian Ocean, the Caribbean and the US – provide supply chain efficiencies and added value for our customers.

We work together with Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. haven Terminals and Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tank Containers to deliver a seamless and efficient ship-to-shore service. We also regularly collaborate with our customers to create bespoke transportation and storage solutions tailored to their needs.

Tanker Trading

Our tanker trading team consists of our chartering and ship-operations experts. The chartering team provides global commercial management services for the Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers fleet and is recognised throughout the industry for its leadership, experience and dedication to customer service.

Ship management

Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers’ ship owning team provides technical management services for all our ships, making sure that our fleet meets or exceeds the rigorous standards set by our customers, classification societies, flag authorities and by Straits Tankers PTE. LTD. Tankers itself.
All of the key functions in ship owning – safety and marine services, crewing, fleet management, risk and insurance, vetting, newbuilding and technical projects – work in unison toward this goal.
We manage most of our ships throughout their complete life cycle, from design specification, through construction, operational life, dry-docking, midlife modernisation, life extensions and, ultimately, recycling. This approach allows us to achieve the highest standards of operation, maintenance and repair – well above industry averages.
