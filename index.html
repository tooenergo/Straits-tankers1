why choose us

We are a long-term investor and manager of businesses in bulk-liquid logistics and sustainable logistics.

Straits Tankers PTE. LTD.'s diverse portfolio enables us to provide safe, high-quality services in all areas of bulk-liquid logistics, storage and the distribution of bulk liquids and chemicals.


We leverage our knowledge and experience within and across these industries to deliver local and global solutions for our customers and generate value for our stakeholders.
